Evidence File 01

Grade Went Up. Recovery Went Down.

As mill throughput was pushed, grind size drifted from 150 µm to 175 µm (P80). Coarser particles leave copper locked inside gangue — it never floats, never reports to concentrate. Recovery fell 3.5 points even as head grade rose.

Evidence File 02

The Tailings Nobody Reads

Tailings copper grade has crept from 0.13% to 0.21% — yet it is sampled by hand just once a shift, with results back 8 hours later. By the time anyone sees a bad assay, 10,000 tonnes of ore have already passed through at that loss.
